About Me

Hello there! My name is Aedan.

I grew up in Bolivia, South America, where I learnt Spanish as a second language when I was young, which is said to help when you go to learn other languages, maybe programming langages are the same? Living in Bolivia growing up, I also gained great perspective on the wider world.

I love learning new things, resulting in countless hobbies, and a wide breadth of understanding in various fields. I have been coding in some fashion for around 8 years, on many different projects. I started out with game engines, and making small games for my friends and I to play, and eventually found my way to working on a startup for a businessman, and then creating an app that I always wanted to exist. I have also gotten very good at learning over the years, making it easier for me to pick up new things. I am passionate about building software to solve real world problems, and want to make a difference in the world with what time I have been given.

I am currently studying at IOD to fill in any gaps my journey may have left, and give me a solid foundation to take into the industry.
